About Our Organization

Serving the Community Since 1995

Ghonapara Islami Samaz Kallan Parishad is a non-political, non-profit voluntary organization established in 1995. We are dedicated to serving humanity regardless of race, religion, or background through various charitable and social welfare initiatives.

Our Key Activities:

  • Helping the poor and helpless
  • Free blood donation campaigns
  • Assistance to poor meritorious students
  • Social development activities
  • Distribution of Iftar in Ramadan
  • Annual religious mahfil organization

Our mission is to create positive change in society through compassion, service, and faith-based initiatives. We believe in empowering communities and helping those in need with dignity and respect.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can join us by visiting our office during working hours or contacting us through our Facebook page. We welcome volunteers who share our vision of serving the community. No special qualifications are needed - just a willingness to help others.
We accept donations through bank transfer, mobile banking, and in-person at our office. You can donate money, clothes, food items, or blood. Contact us at +8801746942468 for our bank account details or visit our donation page for more information.
We organize blood donation camps quarterly and during emergency needs. Our major blood donation events are usually before Ramadan and Eid-ul-Azha when demand is highest. Follow our Facebook page for upcoming blood donation schedules.
We provide educational assistance to meritorious students from poor families. You need to submit an application with your academic records and family income proof at our office. Our committee reviews applications monthly and selects recipients based on need and merit.
You can participate either as a volunteer to help distribute iftar or as a donor to sponsor iftar for individuals or families. Contact us at least one month before Ramadan to coordinate. We distribute iftar packets at mosques and to needy families throughout the holy month.
